Websites Every College Student Should Know

A List of Our Favorite Websites

Knowing what professors to choose can be tricky but using Rate My Professor will help you have the best professors at your school.



Creating flash cards and storing all your notes in different places can be tricky, but Quizlet is a free website and app that allows you to create flashcards so you can store your notes in one place and study on the go.



TED Talks are short videos that range on various topics so in less than half an hour you can learn something new and become inspired.



Paper coupons are inconvenient, but Groupon is a digital platform that has a wide variety of coupons so you can save money on your purchases.


Graphing calculators are expensive, but Desmos is a free online graphing calculator.


Textbooks are expensive, but you can save money using Open Stax, a website that provides free textbook pdfs.
