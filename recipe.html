No-Bake Peanut Butter Bar

Breakfast Snack Dessert

Ingredients:

Filling:

Frosting:


Directions:

For the filling: Grease the pan with some butter

Mix together the butter and chunky peanut butter until smooth. Stir in the sugar a cup at a time.

Add the vanilla wafers to a food processor and process until they are in crumbs. Then add the crumbs and mini chocolate chips to the peanut butter mixture. Spread it evenly into the buttered pan and set aside.

For the frosting: Using a double boiler or a microwave, melt the chocolate chips and creamy peanut butter. Allow to cool for a couple of minutes, then pour over the base. Spread the frosting out evenly and sprinkle over the chopped peanuts.

Refrigerate for at least 1 hour, cut and enjoy!.

Microwave Risotto With Ham, Cheese, & Peas

Lunch Dinner

Ingredients:


Directions:

In a 2-quart microwavable bowl, place 2 tablespoons of the butter, the onion, garlic and pepper. Cover tightly with plastic wrap; microwave on high for 2 to 4 minutes or until onion is soft. Add rice; stir to coat with butter. Cover; set aside.

Place broth in a 4-cup microwavable measuring cup. Microwave uncovered on High 5 minutes or until hot. Pour into rice mixture; stir. Cover tightly with plastic wrap; microwave on High 5 minutes.

Stir mixture. Cover tightly; microwave on Medium (50%) 12 minutes. Stir in ham and frozen peas; continue to cook on Medium (50%) 5 to 7 minutes longer or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed.

Stir in remaining 2 tablespoons butter and the cheese. Enjoy!

Mac N Cheese in A Mug

Lunch Dinner

Ingredients:


Directions:

Put macaroni in a mug and pour in water. (You may want to add more or less water depending on mug size, but there should be more than enough to completely cover noodles).

Microwave on high for 8 minutes pausing at the 5 and 2 minute mark to stir and add more water if necessary.

Cook until al dente.

Add cheese to pasta mixing with the small amount of water remaining at bottom of mug

If cheese does not completely melt, pop mug back in microwave for 30-60 seconds

Stir then enjoy!

Cinnamon Apple and Banana Oatmeal

Breakfast

Ingredients:


Directions:

Begin by cleaning the apple with water.Then slice the apple into small cubes.

Take your banana and peel it. Then cut the banana into small pieces.

Take your two packs of instant oatmeal and place them in a bowl. Add 1 tbsp of butter to the bowl as well as the required milk / water on the instant oatmeal package. Microwave the oatmeal for 1.5 - 2 minutes.

Once oatmeal is cooked, add your sliced apples and bananas. Top everything off with as much cinnamon of your choice.

Stir ingredients together and enjoy!
